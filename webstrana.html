About Me

Hello to whoever visiting this page! My name is Mihaela, I'm 21 and I live in Skopje, Macedonia. I'm in my third year of studies towards a bachelor's degree in Telecommunication and Information engineering at FEEIT Skopje.

I would describe myself as a very enthusiastic student and I’ve been that way since I was little. I have an insatiable thirst for knowledge! Ok, maybe I was (am) just a regular nerd… I have tendencies of analysing every single detail at everything. All in all, I am pretty much an organized person. Oh wait… I see… I didn’t tell you this was my “bragging section”. I am not even sorry! For funnier and more enjoyable stuff click here.

Anyway, in my spare time I enjoy reading books, watching movies and listening to good music. Although the “music part” is not just in my spare time. I also love working out. I like to go jogging occasionally, that’s not my favourite type of exercise though. But I really enjoy hiking and going for long walks.

Books

I LOOOOVE reading books. Over the years it became like a regular habit of mine. A day doesn’t go by without going through at least 9-10 pages. On a good day there are many, many more. I’ve read some really good books, and some really bad ones too which I successfully survived till the last page. But I never give up on them. Slowly but surely. Maybe next time I would know what not to pick.
*FUN FACT* Just now, I found out that a lot of people read the last page first! Why would someone spoil such an adventure for themselves?! Okay, maybe it allows you to look for deeper meaning while reading… I don’t know, I think one can multitask.
